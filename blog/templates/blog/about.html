Maharashtrians celebrate their New Year on the Pratipada Tithi (first day) of Chaitra month, Shukla Paksha (waxing phase of the Moon)

The New Year day is known as Gudi Padwa

On this day, people hoist a Gudi (a collection of items) or a flag outside their door or window
